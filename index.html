Collaborate with‍

Anyone, ANYWHERE,

in REAL-TIME

Muse is a music collaboration app that connects you with anyone in the world.

With Muse’s powerful suite of tools, you can use your favorite DAW to collaborate online as if you’re in the same studio together, without the inconvenience of downloading multiple apps that pull you out of your workflow.

FEATURES

With Muse, you can collaborate online as you would in person. Share multiple high quality audio streams with the session and record audio over the network. Quickly exchange ideas by sharing files or playing MIDI over the network. Take control of the streamlined process by seamlessly moving between Muse and the DAW you're used to.

GET THE APP
Collaborate in Real-Time

Communicate with each other, share any audio device, hear what others are making, share your screen or control someone else’s computer. Everything you need for efficient collaboration, all in one place.

Share High Quality Audio

Share multiple audio devices with the option to stream high quality for recording. You can even share audio from your computer or DAW so collaborators can hear what's coming through your speakers like they're right next to you.

Record Audio Over the Network

Easily record any device being shared with the session directly into your DAW. The Muse Audio Driver acts as a virtual audio interface to pass audio from one app to another, allowing you to record audio from anywhere on your computer. Learn more

File Sharing

Quickly exchange ideas by dragging files into Muse’s chat box, or from the chat box into your DAW. Depending on your software, you can drag files directly from your DAW, into Muse.

Play & Record MIDI Over the Network

Share your MIDI devices with the session so that your collaborators can receive and record incoming MIDI into their DAW. Learn more

Streamlined Process

Muse’s quick actions allow you to seamlessly move between your DAW and the Muse app. Pin the sidebar to always be visible and have access to user inputs and file sharing, or move the sidebar out of view to focus on your project. Conveniently mute or solo shared devices for more granular control over your session.
